Common Questions

What is Alpha Phi?

 Alpha Phi is a women's fraternity that focuses on sisterhood, service, scholarship, leadership, loyalty, and character development.

What is Alpha Phi's Philanthropy?

 Alpha Phi focuses on women's heart health. 1 in 5 women will pass from some form of heart disease. We strive to get these women the care and support they need.

What is the Alpha Phi Chi Chapter?

 The Alpha Phi Chi Chapter is located at the University of Montana in Missoula, MT. We are the 22nd Chapter of Alpha Phi founded and currently have 80 active members.
